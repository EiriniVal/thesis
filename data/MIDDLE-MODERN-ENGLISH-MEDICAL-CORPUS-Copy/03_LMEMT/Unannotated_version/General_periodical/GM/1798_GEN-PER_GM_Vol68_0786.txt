[}234. A Treatise on the Teeth, with an accu- 
rate Idea of their Structure. By the Che- 
valier Ruspini. }]
THE Chevalier Ruspini has long 
been celebrated for his skill in treating 
all disorders of the teeth and gums. In 
this treatise he has evinced much theo- 
retical knowledge on the subject. He 
has added a few cases of cures effected 
by his dentifrice powder and tincture; 
as also those effected by his styptic.
